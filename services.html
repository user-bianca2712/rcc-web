What we do

Our comprehensive approach to fighting TB involves education,support, and advocacy at multiple levels.

Public Awareness Campaigns

We conduct workshops,seminars,and information sessions in communities,schools, and workplaces to educate people about TB prevention,symptoms,and treatment.

  • Community health talks
  • School education programs
  • Workplace wellness sessions
  • Media campaigns

Educational resources

We provide easy-to-understand materials in multiple languages to ensure everyone has access to accurate TB information.

  • Pamphlets and brochures
  • Infographics and posters
  • Digital content for social media
  • Multilingual resources

TB symptoms

Persistent Cough

Lasting 3 weeks or longer,sometimes with blood

chest pain

Pain while breathing or coughing

Weight loss

Unintentional and significant

Fatigue & weakness

Constant tiredness and lack of energy

Fever and chills

Especially in the afternoon

Night sweats

Drenching sweats during sleep

Support group linkage

We connect TB patients and their families with local support groups to provide emotional support and practical assistance throughout the treatment journey

Advocacy

We work with healthcare providers and government agencies to improve TB services and ensure testing and treatment are accessible to all
