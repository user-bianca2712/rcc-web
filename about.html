Our History

TB Aware Now was founded in 2018 by a group of healthcare workers and TB survivors who witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of misinformation and stigma surrounding Tuberculosis.What started as a small community initiative has grown into a recognized non-profit organization working across multiple regions.

Our Mission

"To eliminate TB as a public health threat through education,advocacy, and community support."

Our Vision

"A world where no one suffers or dies from Tuberculosis."
